Founded in 2015 by visionary filmmaker Riya Kapoor, Noir Studio is based in Mumbai, India. The studio began as a small experimental lab and quickly grew into a creative powerhouse. Known for fashion films, indie shorts, and bold brand campaigns, Noir Studio has won multiple awards including the Cannes Creative Award 2018, Berlin Art Direction Prize 2019, and the Indie Film Excellence Award 2021. Our mission is to challenge conventions, embrace expressive color, push boundaries in typography, and craft experiences that linger long after the first glance.
